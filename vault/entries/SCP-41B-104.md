# Retention Order — Consignment Lot 41B/108

**FORM 41-R · SITE-41B DEEP-RECORDS ANNEX**

**LOT:** This order binds the consignment logged on the intake slip [[SCP-41B-101]] in
its entirety, enumerated: the slip itself; the personnel action [[SCP-41B-102]]; the
containment amendment [[SCP-41B-103]]; and the withheld record the slip's manifest
carries without a title. An order that names its lot governs the lot's copies wherever
they sit, per the manual; the annex intends the phrase literally and includes the copies
it transmits.

**COPYING DISCIPLINE:** The lot is filed in ⟦a1⟧, per standard practice. The annex
reminds the receiving station that the discipline takes a name, not a description, and
that the name is the ordinary one — the same practice every Stack I holding has been
filed under since conversion. Three copies, struck together, distributed to separate
stacks; the copies are compared at audit, and a disagreement between them is a finding,
to be reported and never reconciled.

**DISTRIBUTION:** First copy retained, Stack I. Second copy, Stack II, filed with the
audit calendar. Third copy transmitted — the receiving station is holding it.

**REVIEW CYCLE:** Annual, on the audit calendar of the acting archivist. The annex notes
that the review has been kept every year, including the years for which the annex has no
other records of any kind.

**ISSUING OFFICER:** Records office, by the hand of the acting archivist appointed at
[[SCP-41B-102]].

> third copy is yours now. the discipline is not superstition — three decay slower than
> one, and when they disagree, the disagreement is the datum. count the copies.  — r.h.
