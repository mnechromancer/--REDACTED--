# REF-06 — Term Appointment Packet (this desk's copy)

**Conditions of temporary employment, records section · Site-81C · retain for the
duration of the appointment**

## Appointment

The bearer is appointed archivist, temporary, term. The appointment carries a terminal
credential, a desk, and no clearance beyond the records floor. It confers no authority to
originate correspondence with other sites, to certify records, or to decline an assigned
lot. Compensation is fortnightly, in arrears. The appointment ends when the assignment
ends, and the assignment does not end when the assigned work does; consult Records
Supervision, who will not know either.

## Conditions

The temporary archivist will process the material assigned by Records Supervision using
the station's standard catalogue terminal. Training is not provided. The manuals on the
reference shelf are current, or were when the shelf was assembled; the shelf is the floor
copy and volumes are not to leave the records room.

The station holds no anomalous material. Staff experiencing anomalous effects are
reminded of this in the first instance.

## Housekeeping

Coffee is available from the corridor urn on a contribution basis. The parking apron is
north of the annex; the south lot belongs to the motor pool, which defends it. The east
corridor fire door is not to be propped, whatever Facilities was doing there last week.
Lost credentials are replaced once.

## Acknowledgment

The undersigned acknowledges the conditions above, acknowledges that the station's
records-retention practices apply to station paperwork only and not to personal effects,
and acknowledges having been told nothing further.
