# Item #: SCP-41B-002

**Object Class:** Safe

## Special Containment Procedures

SCP-41B-002 is an accession number with no associated physical holding. The number is to be
retained in the active register and audited annually alongside the originating intake, with which it shares an
intake. Addenda filed against SCP-41B-002 are to be preserved as received; they are not to be
consolidated, and the accession is not to be retired while addenda continue to accrue.
SCP-41B-002 shares its intake lot with [[SCP-41B-001]].

## Description

SCP-41B-002 is an accession number assigned, on the originating manifest, to ⟦a1⟧. No item
was ever logged into the vault against it. Despite holding nothing, the accession continues
to receive addenda — incident notes, interview fragments, audit corrections — at a rate
indistinguishable from an active holding, each filed in good order by personnel who do not,
on later inquiry, recall filing them.

The accession passes audit as present every year. The presence audited is the file, not an
object: there is no object. On the matter of the audit itself, ⟦a2⟧ — the reconciliation
against the holding record closes clean each cycle. The only note ever raised against it is
informal and undocketed: two clerks reconciling the register once remarked that their copies
of the prior year's audit seemed to disagree on the count of addenda, but neither filed it,
and the next audit closed clean like all the others.

## Addendum 002.1 — Liaison Note

A note appears in the file dated to the year of intake, countersigned T. Andrade (Site-41),
addressed as though in reply to a query SCP-41B-002 had raised — though an accession number
cannot raise a query, and no outgoing correspondence is on record. The note concerns that
same accession lot and reads, in part, that the consignment "arrived complete and was logged short."
No further Andrade correspondence is filed after 1968.
