# Personnel Action Memorandum — Records Office

**FORM 41-P · SITE-41B DEEP-RECORDS ANNEX**

**ACTION:** Appointment, acting.

**NAME AND POST:** R. ⟦a1⟧, records clerk, Stack II, is appointed **acting archivist**
of the deep-records annex, with custody of the reading rota, the audit calendar, and the
margin privilege.

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** Upon the vacancy of the records office, which the annex dates to the
custody reorganization and declines to date more precisely.

**AUTHORITY:** I. Vogel, Director. Signed in the Director's ink; the signature has not
been witnessed, the Director's office not appearing on any current blueprint.

**REMARKS:** The appointee is the clerk who has handled intake transcription and audit
filing on Stack II since before the reorganization, and is the last member of the records
office on the active roster. The post of records officer remains formally held by its
founding occupant, whose detachment to catalogue work in the deep stacks the annex has no
record of ending. The acting archivist inherits the officer's duties but not the office;
correspondence is to be routed to the records office generally, per the directory
practice of receiving stations.

The margin privilege conveyed above licenses the acting archivist to annotate holdings in
their own hand. The annex notes, for the record, that annotation is working material and
enjoys no protection under the annex's retention practices.
